Mulank · The Birth Number

Born of the Sun

The number of the leader — radiant, original, and built to stand at the front of things.

Ravi, your Mulank is 1 — the number of beginnings, governed by the Sun itself. Just as the Sun does not ask permission to rise, you carry an inner authority that others sense before you speak. You were not made to follow a path laid by someone else; you were made to lay one.

Your strength is your independence and your clarity of will. When you decide, you move. People are drawn to that certainty, and in your best years it makes you a natural leader at home, at work, and among friends. The Sun gives you vitality, ambition, and a warmth that lifts the people around you.

Bhagyank · The Destiny Number

The Path of Venus

6
Ruled by
Shukra · Venus
The number of love, beauty, and responsibility — the destiny of one who builds harmony around them.

Where your Mulank is who you are, your Bhagyank is where life is taking you. Yours is 6 — the number of Venus, of beauty, devotion, and care. Life will repeatedly place you at the centre of a home, a team, or a community that needs holding together, and you will find that you are remarkably good at it.

Your destiny rewards you through relationships and through the things you create with love — a family, a craft, a beautiful space, a loyal circle. Comfort, art, and affection are not distractions for you; they are the very road your fate runs along. Money and recognition tend to arrive through people who trust you.

The shadow of 6 is over-giving — carrying everyone, pleasing everyone, until little is left for you. Your destiny asks you to love without losing yourself, and to receive as gracefully as you give.

Where Destiny Favours You

  • Family, home, and community
  • Art, design, and beauty
  • Counselling and caregiving roles
  • Loyal, lasting relationships

What Destiny Asks of You

  • Set loving boundaries
  • Avoid people-pleasing
  • Let others care for you too
  • Choose peace over perfection
